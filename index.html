Why it works

The design mirrors your logo instead of fighting it.

The black base keeps the page dramatic, the charcoal cards create clean reading areas, and the gold-amber highlights pull directly from the candle flame and circular brushstroke in the logo.

Visual direction details

  • Background stays nearly black for cinematic depth.
  • Cards use dark charcoal grey for contrast and legibility.
  • Buttons and accents use amber-gold gradients and soft glow.
  • Animated embers add movement without overwhelming the content.
